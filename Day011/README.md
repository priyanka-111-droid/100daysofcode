## Our Blackjack House Rules 
-----------------------------
### 1.The deck is unlimited in size. 
### 2.There are no jokers. 
### 3.The Jack/Queen/King all count as 10.
### 4.The the Ace can count as 11 or 1.

### 8.The computer is the dealer.
